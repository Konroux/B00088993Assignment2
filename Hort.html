Heads or Tails Video Assignment

Description

When I was in the ideas stage of this assignment I quickly decided I wanted to tell a simple story but still have it be entertaining and not completely predictable. In truth the story I tell in my video is loosely based on a dream I had not that long ago. I altered it with the intention of conveying a theme and story arc as dreams often don't have a clear beginning and end. I wanted to tell a story of bad decisions and display a theme of manipulation and karma. At this stage I had a pretty good idea of what I wanted the film to look like and so I wrote up the general scene by scene that I wanted to include for the sake of organisation. I knew I needed two main actors and decided it would be easier if one of them was me. So i had another friend holding the camera for some scenes in which I demonstrated how the camera should move in order to get it perfect. I used location within 20 minutes of my house which made costume changes and travel costs not a problem. The microphone on my camera is not great and me and my friend aren't exactly actors so I decided that there would be no speech or dialogue in my film apart from 2 lines in which I subtitled. Another reason for this was that I didn't feel It needed and talking.
