What Do I Do?

My name is Conor Diggin and I am currently a Creative Digital Media in the Institute of Technology Blanchardstown. I was born in London, England however I have lived here in Ireland for 15 years. My main interest in media is in the film industry but I also enjoy photography and web design. So feel free to check out my collection of work in this portfolio. If you would be interested on working on a project with me or have a question, my contact details are displayed on the "contact me" page. Thank You.
